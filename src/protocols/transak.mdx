## What is Transak?

Transak is a cryptocurrency on-ramp infrastructure that simplifies the process of purchasing digital assets using local fiat currency. It offers a seamless and compliant solution for applications to integrate fiat-to-crypto on-ramps, providing users with a convenient way to buy cryptocurrencies.

## Key Features of Transak

Cryptocurrency On-Ramp: Transak facilitates fiat-to-crypto transactions, allowing users to easily purchase digital assets.

Payment Methods: Supports a wide range of payment methods to enhance user accessibility.

Compliance: Provides a compliant solution for applications, ensuring a secure and legal onboarding process.

## Why Choose Transak?

Transak aims to streamline the user onboarding experience for blockchain applications, making it easy for users to enter the cryptocurrency space using their local fiat currency.
