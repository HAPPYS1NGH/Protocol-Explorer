## What is Rarible?

Rarible - NFT Marketplace for Brands, Communites and Traders.An aggregated NFT marketplace for Ethereum NFTs and Polygon NFTs powers brands, collections and creator marketplaces.

## Key Features

NFT Marketplace: Rarible serves as an NFT marketplace where users can mint, buy, and sell digital collectibles without requiring coding skills.

Creation and Minting: Users can create and mint NFTs on the Rarible platform, allowing content creators to tokenize their creations such as books, music albums, or movies.

Rarible Clone Script to Create an NFT Marketplace: User can use this powerful tool to create a marketplace similar to Rarible.

Blockchain Security: Rarible secures digital collectibles using blockchain technology, ensuring the authenticity and ownership of NFTs.

ERC-721 and ERC-1155 Support: Rarible supports both ERC-721 and ERC-1155 standards for NFT creation and trading.

Community Governance Model: Rarible is shifting towards becoming a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO), where all decision rights will belong to the platform users through community governance.

Creator Verification and Moderation: Rarible offers features for creator verification and moderation, ensuring the quality and authenticity of NFTs on the platform.
