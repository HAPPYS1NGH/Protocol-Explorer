## What is Gelato?

Gelato is web3’s decentralized backend, empowering builders to create augmented smart contracts that are automated, gasless, and off-chain aware. It operates seamlessly on major EVM-compatible blockchains, including Ethereum, Polygon, Fantom, Arbitrum, BNB Chain, Optimism, and more.

## Gelato Services

1. Web3 Functions: Connect your smart contracts to off-chain data & computation by running decentralized cloud functions.
  
2. Automate: Automate your smart contracts by executing transactions automatically in a reliable, developer-friendly & decentralized manner.
  
3. Relay: Give your users access to reliable, robust, and scalable gasless transactions via a simple-to-use API.
  
4. Gasless Wallet: A powerful SDK that enables developers to provide a world-class UX by combining Gelato Relay + Safe's Smart Contract Wallet to enable Account Abstraction.

## Key Features of Gelato

- Decentralized Backend: Gelato serves as a decentralized backend for building automated, gasless, and off-chain aware smart contracts.
  
- EVM-Compatible Blockchains: Gelato seamlessly operates on all major EVM-compatible blockchains, ensuring broad compatibility.
  
- Comprehensive Services: Gelato offers a suite of services, including Web3 Functions, Automation, Relay, and Gasless Wallet, catering to diverse developer needs.
  
- Developer-Friendly: Gelato provides a reliable, developer-friendly platform for creating augmented smart contracts.

## Who is Gelato For?
