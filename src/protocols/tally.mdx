It simplifies onchain governance, enabling users to delegate voting power, create or pass proposals, manage protocols, and upgrade smart contractsâ€”all onchain. Tally supports DAOs across various chains, including Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Avalanche, BNB Chain, Gnosis, Base, Moonbeam, and Scroll.

## Tally for Operators

- DAO Hub: Build a comprehensive hub for your DAO, providing contributors with a centralized platform for information and participation in governance.

- Proposal Management: Seamlessly create and manage proposals, streamlining the decision-making process within your organization.

## Tally for Voters

Unified Voting Platform: Access a unified platform for understanding and participating in proposal voting.

Unique Voting Identity: Create and share your unique voting identity for your DAOs, establishing a personalized presence.

Voting Power Management: Manage and track your voting power efficiently, ensuring a transparent and effective voting process.

Participation in Proposals: Engage actively by voting on proposals, contributing to the decision-making process.

## Tally for Delegates

Delegate Showcase: Build a profile that highlights your active participation in various DAOs.

Broadcast Influence: Broadcast your influence and proposals to garner support from the community.

Interaction with Proposals: Vote and interact on DAO proposals, leveraging your influence to shape governance decisions.
