## What is Ankr?

Ankr is a decentralized Web3 infrastructure provider, fostering seamless connections between developers, dApps, stakers, and blockchains. Founded in 2017, Ankr has a mission to build the most decentralized and scalable Web3 infrastructure. The native cryptocurrency, ANKR, serves as a medium of exchange on the Ankr network, enabling users to pay for services like running a node, accessing applications, staking, and participating in governance proposals.

## Key Features

- Web3 Infrastructure: Ankr provides decentralized infrastructure, facilitating efficient communication between developers, dApps, stakers, and blockchains.

- Cryptocurrency (ANKR): ANKR is the native cryptocurrency, used for payments, staking, governance voting, and accessing custom solutions on the Ankr network.

- Scalable and Decentralized: Ankr focuses on building scalable and decentralized Web3 infrastructure to contribute to a more private and secure decentralized internet.

## ANKR Cryptocurrency Use Cases

- Payments: ANKR is used as a medium of exchange to pay for services on the Ankr network, including the monthly fee for running a node.

- Staking: ANKR serves as a staking asset, allowing users to participate in securing the network and earning rewards.

- Governance: Holders can use ANKR to vote on governance proposals, actively participating in the decision-making process.
