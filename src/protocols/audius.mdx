## What is Audius?

Audius is a decentralized music streaming and sharing platform that redefines the music industry by empowering content creators. Launched in 2018, Audius enables artists and listeners to connect directly without subscription fees, providing a platform where creators have full control over their music.

## Key Features of Audius

- Decentralized Platform: Audius operates on a decentralized model, removing intermediaries and giving power back to music creators.

- AUDIO Token: The native token, AUDIO, fuels the Audius ecosystem, offering monetary rewards and ownership opportunities.

## How Audius Works

Artists can publish their music on Audius, interact with their fans, and maintain ownership. Users can listen to music without subscription fees and even earn AUDIO tokens as rewards for sharing playlists.

## Benefits of Audius

- Empowering Content Creators: Audius empowers content creators by providing a direct connection with their audience and ensuring control over their music.

- Monetary Rewards: Users can earn AUDIO tokens as rewards for actively participating in the Audius ecosystem.
