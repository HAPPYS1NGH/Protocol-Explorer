## What is Developer DAO?

Developer DAO is a developer-owned and governed community learning and building for Web3.

## What do we do?

Developer DAO provides opportunities and incentives for builders of all levels to learn about Web3 and build projects that impact the world.

## GETTING STARTED IN THE DAO

Much of what Developer DAO does do is free and open to everyone as a public good. The first step to tap into the value the DAO can provide you is connecting with us on essential channels. They update and announce everything.
