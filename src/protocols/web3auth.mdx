## What is Web3Auth?

Web3Auth is a pluggable wallet infrastructure designed for Web3 wallets and applications. It streamlines the onboarding process for both mainstream and crypto-native users, ensuring a seamless experience in under a minute. Key features include:

## Versatile Authentication

Web3Auth supports all OAuth-based login systems, providing compatibility with various mainstream and crypto-native user authentication methods.

## Seamless Onboarding

Offer your users a seamless onboarding experience, allowing them to choose authentication methods they are most comfortable with.

## Platform Compatibility

Web3Auth is compatible with both web and mobile native platforms, ensuring a consistent and user-friendly experience across devices.

## Pluggable Wallet Infrastructure

Integrate Web3Auth effortlessly into your applications, providing a pluggable solution for wallet infrastructure in the Web3 ecosystem.
